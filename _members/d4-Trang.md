Born in Hanoi, Vietnam, I completed my Master’s degree in South Korea with a focus on nutrition. My research interests lie in nutritional epidemiology, particularly obesity, dietary factors, and metabolic biomarkers in relation to chronic disease risk. I am interested in using population-based data to understand diet–disease relationships and inform public health strategies. Outside research, I enjoy learning languages, doing crafts, and going to the gym.
