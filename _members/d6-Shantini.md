Shanthini Rajan completed her Bachelor’s degree in Psychological Science and is currently pursuing a Master of Applied Research in Social Sciences in Singapore. Her research interests lie in behavioral and social science, particularly in understanding attitudes and behaviors related to eating behaviour, well-being, and digital environments such as social media. She is interested in using mixed-methods approaches to explore how psychological and social factors influence health-related behaviors and decision-making. Her work focuses on applying research methods to better understand real-world social and health issues. Outside of research, she enjoys playing badminton, travelling, and doing pottery.
