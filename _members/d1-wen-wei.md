I received my bachelor’s degree in biomedical engineering from National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University in Taiwan, with research interests in biomedical informatics, digital health, and data-driven healthcare. My work focuses on cardiometabolic and behavioral health, including weight management, and explores how digital health technologies and artificial intelligence can support the prevention and management of chronic conditions. I am particularly interested in improving how health and behavioral data are collected, integrated, and analyzed to generate meaningful insights for healthcare research and intervention design. Outside of work, I enjoy playing badminton and doing crafts.
