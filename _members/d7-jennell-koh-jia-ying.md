I am a nursing researcher interested in cardiometabolic and behavioral health, particularly in how digital health and artificial intelligence can support the prevention and management of these conditions. My research explores whether mHealth interventions grounded in behavioral theories and artificial intelligence can improve cardiometabolic health among individuals with metabolic syndrome and whether these interventions are feasible in real-world settings. Outside of work, my colleagues and I recently started running together, and it has turned into a regular weekly routine. I still really enjoy good food though, especially bubble tea!
